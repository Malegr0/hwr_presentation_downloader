Welcome to the PDF creator for BBB presentations!

How to use it.

  1. Get the link of one pdf site.
  2. Copy the link in the quotation marks "http://malegro.ddns.net:5007/?link=" and put it in the address field.
  3. Put the link of the pdf site behind the address you put in the address field.
  4. Press enter, download the pdf document and have fun!
